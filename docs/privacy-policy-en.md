# **Privacy Policy: Pun Thai Coffee Co., Ltd.**

**Last Updated:** July 1, 2024

**Effective Date:** June 1, 2022

Pun Thai Coffee Co., Ltd. (“the Company”) values and respects your privacy and recognizes the importance of personal data protection and security in accordance with legal and international standards. This policy informs customers, partners, and related persons about how the company collects, uses, and discloses personal data.

## **1\. Key Definitions**

* **Personal Data:** Information relating to a natural person that allows for direct or indirect identification, excluding deceased persons.  
* **Sensitive Personal Data:** Data such as race, religion, health records, criminal history, and biological data (e.g., face recognition, fingerprints) which require explicit consent.  
* **Data Subject:** The individual to whom the personal data belongs.

## **2\. Data Collection**

The Company collects various types of data, including:

* **Personal details:** Name, ID number, passport number, nationality, and age.  
* **Contact Information:** Address, phone number, and email.  
* **Financial Information:** Bank account numbers and credit card details.  
* **Health Information:** Medical history and treatment records for service users.  
* **Technical Data:** CCTV recordings, voice recordings, and website usage data.

## **3\. Purposes of Data Processing**

The Company processes data for the following reasons:

* **Contractual Necessity:** To fulfill purchase agreements, delivery, and payments.  
* **Legitimate Interest:** For quality control, fraud prevention, and security monitoring.  
* **Legal Compliance:** To comply with government orders, court warrants, and tax laws.  
* **Marketing (with Consent):** To provide promotions, discounts, and personalized offers.

## **4\. Data Sharing and Disclosure**

The Company may disclose personal data to:

* Affiliates within the PTG Group.  
* Business partners and service providers for marketing and analysis (with consent).  
* External auditors, legal advisors, and government authorities as required by law.

## **5\. Data Retention and Security**

* **Retention Period:** Data is generally kept for a maximum of **10 years** after the end of the business relationship or the last contact.  
* **Security Measures:** The Company implements international standards to prevent loss, unauthorized access, or misuse of data.

## **6\. Your Rights as a Data Subject**

You have the right to:

* Access and request a copy of your personal data.  
* Request data portability in electronic formats.  
* Object to data collection or processing.  
* Request deletion, destruction, or anonymization of your data.  
* Withdraw your consent at any time (subject to legal/contractual limits).  
* Request correction of inaccurate data.

## **7\. Cookies**

The Company uses cookies to improve website functionality and user experience. More details can be found at: [https://www.ptgenergy.co.th/AboutPTG/Cookiepolicy](https://www.ptgenergy.co.th/AboutPTG/Cookiepolicy).
