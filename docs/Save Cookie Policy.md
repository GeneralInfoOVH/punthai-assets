## **1\. What are Cookies?**

Cookies are small files that may be installed and/or stored on your device (such as a computer, mobile phone, or tablet) when you visit a website. These cookies are sent back to the originating website each time you visit it or to other websites that recognize the same cookies.

Cookies serve various purposes, including enabling a website to function efficiently, enhancing performance, and collecting or tracking personal data for website improvement and targeted marketing.
